How to Play

  • Red moves first - Click on a piece to select it
  • Move diagonally forward to an adjacent empty square
  • Jump over opponent pieces to capture them
  • Kings (_pieces with crowns) can move and capture in any diagonal direction
  • When a piece reaches the opposite end, it becomes a King
  • Capture as many pieces as possible in one turn if available
